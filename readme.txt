MimaPay for WooCommerce allows your store in any African country to accept secure payments from multiple global payment channels.

== Description ==

MimaPay makes it easy for businesses in Africa to accept secure payments from multiple local and global payment channels. Integrate MimaPay with your store today, and let your customers pay you with their choice of methods.

With MimaPay for WooCommerce, you can accept payments via:

* Credit/Debit Cards — Visa, Mastercard, American Express
* Bank transfer
* Wise
* Many more coming soon

= Why MimaPay? =

* Start receiving payments instantly—go from sign-up to your first real transaction in as little as 15 minutes
* Simple, transparent pricing—no hidden charges or fees
* Modern, seamless payment experience via the MimaPay Checkout — 
* Advanced fraud detection
* Understand your customers better through a simple and elegant dashboard
* Access to attentive, empathetic customer support 24/7
* Free updates as we launch new features and payment options
* Clearly documented APIs to build your custom payment experiences

Over 10,000 businesses of all sizes in Africa rely on MimaPay's suite of products to receive payments and make payouts seamlessly. Sign up on [mimapay.africa/signup](https://mimapay.africa/signup) to get started.


= Note =

This plugin is meant to be used by merchants in Africa.

= Plugin Features =

*   __Accept payment__ via Mastercard, Visa, Bank Transfer,
*   __Seamless integration__ into the WooCommerce checkout page. Accept payment directly on your site
*   __Refunds__ from the WooCommerce order details page. Refund an order directly from the order details page



== Installation ==

*   Go to __WordPress Admin__ > __Plugins__ > __Add New__ from the left-hand menu
*   In the search box type __MimaPay WooCommerce Payment Gateway__
*   Click on Install now when you see __MimaPay WooCommerce Payment Gateway__ to install the plugin
*   After installation, __activate__ the plugin.


= MimaPay Setup and Configuration =
*   Go to __WooCommerce > Settings__ and click on the __Payments__ tab
*   You'll see MimaPay listed along with your other payment methods. Click __Set Up__
*   On the next screen, configure the plugin. There is a selection of options on the screen. Read what each one does below.

1. __Enable/Disable__ - Check this checkbox to Enable MimaPay on your store's checkout
2. __Title__ - This will represent MimaPay on your list of Payment options during checkout. It guides users to know which option to select to pay with MimaPay. __Title__ is set to "Debit/Credit Cards" by default, but you can change it to suit your needs.
3. __Test Mode__ - Check this to enable test mode. When selected, the fields in step six will say "Test" instead of "Live." Test mode enables you to test payments before going live. The orders process with test payment methods, no money is involved so there is no risk. You can uncheck this when your store is ready to accept real payments.
4. __API Keys__ - The next two text boxes are for your MimaPay API keys, which you can get from your MimaPay Dashboard. If you enabled Test Mode in step four, then you'll need to use your test API keys here. Otherwise, you can enter your live keys.
5. Click on __Save Changes__ to update the settings.

If you do not find MimaPay on the Payment method options, please go through the settings again and ensure that:

*   You've checked the __"Enable/Disable"__ checkbox
*   You've entered your __API Keys__ in the appropriate field
*   You've clicked on __Save Changes__ during setup
